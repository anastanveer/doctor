Thank you for taking the time to complete the MSRA Clinical Problem Solving mock papers. They have been designed to replicate the official Clinical Problem Solving exam questions as closely as possible in both content & format.

  • We recommend attempting these mock papers under exam conditions.
  • Each mock paper includes 97 items, and should be completed in 75 minutes.
  • There is no negative marking.
  • The answers and rationale will be presented for review at the end of the paper.

The Revise MSRA mock papers focus on clinchers, recalls and question subject areas known to come up frequently in the MSRA exam, so be sure to review the answers and key learning summaries after completing each paper.
